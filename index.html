About Us

About Norm's Restoration

This family-owned, family-run vehicle restoration shop is a true treasure for enthusiasts of classic automobiles. Their passion lies in the art of customizing vintage vehicles, making them unique and personalized works of automotive art. While they take on various restoration projects, they've honed their expertise in bringing old tractors back to life, paying meticulous attention to every detail. What truly sets them apart is their unwavering commitment to precision. Each project that rolls into their shop is treated with care and dedication, ensuring that the end result is a beautifully customized, fully restored vehicle that embodies the essence of the past.

We bring old vehicles back to life, one detail at a time. It's not just a job; it's our way of preserving history and creating something special for our clients. Don DeSilver CEO-Owner
Our Service

What We Provide To Our Customer

We offer our customers the opportunity to either restore their vehicle to its former glory or create the car of their dreams.

  • Body Restorations

    Repairing and restoring the vehicle's body, including rust removal, dent repair, and metal fabrication.

  • Painting and Finishing work

    Stripping old paint, applying new paint, and ensuring a flawless finish.

  • Diagnostic

    Conducting comprehensive diagnostic services on vehicles, using advanced tools and technology to pinpoint and resolve issues quickly and efficiently.

  • Interior Restoration

    Reupholstering seats, restoring the dashboard, and replacing or refurbishing interior components.
